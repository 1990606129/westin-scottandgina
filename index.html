01 — Overview
Scotch, sun,
& storytelling.

Scott and Gina were invited to The Westin Kierland Resort & Spa in Scottsdale, Arizona for an exclusive scotch tasting experience in partnership with Glenmorangie. The collaboration showcased the resort's renowned Scotch Library —one of the most impressive whiskey collections in the Southwest —alongside curated food pairings and expert-led tasting sessions.

The content captured the unique intersection of luxury resort living and world-class spirits education, positioning the Westin Kierland as a destination for elevated, experience-driven travel. The scotch tasting angle gave audiences a fresh reason to visit beyond the typical pool-and-golf narrative.

"It felt like a really delicious history class in university. Highly recommend booking this experience —the scotch library is incredible."

04 — The Experience
Everything
provided.

A breakdown of the full hosted experience —from the private scotch tasting to the resort amenities —that made this collaboration unique.

Signature Experience
Private Scotch Tasting
An exclusive, guided tasting session in the Westin Kierland's celebrated Scotch Library —featuring a curated selection of Glenmorangie expressions paired with complementary food courses. Led by a dedicated scotch guide who walked through the history, distillation process, and tasting notes of each pour.
Culinary
Curated Food Pairings
Expert-selected food pairings designed to complement each scotch expression —elevating the tasting from a simple spirits experience to a multi-sensory culinary journey. Each course was crafted to highlight different flavor profiles in the whiskey.
Resort
Full Resort Access
Access to the Westin Kierland's 732-acre resort grounds —including the Adventure Water Park with 110-foot waterslide, Kierland Golf Club, pool cabanas, and the award-winning Agave, The Arizona Spa. A full desert luxury experience beyond the tasting.
Location
Scottsdale, Arizona
Set against the dramatic backdrop of the Sonoran Desert in Scottsdale —one of America's top luxury travel destinations. The desert landscape provided stunning visual content and positioned the experience within a broader aspirational travel narrative.
